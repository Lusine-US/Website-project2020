How to Use TikTok

Go to the app store or google play and download TicTok. It is a free app to download. Once downloaded it is very easy to set up an account. You can sign up by linking any other social media apps or by using your email to sign in. After logging in you will be brought to a home page which contains random trending videos. The more content you like the more the home page will be suited to content you like to watch.

From here I clicked onto videos and up popped thousands of art tutorials for me to browse through. Once you find a video you like you click into it to watch. From here you can access the video creators page by swiping right and browse through other videos they have. You can also use the tools to the right of the video (shown on right image) to like, comment and share to other people/platforms.

How to post a Tic Tok To create your own Tic Tok you need to tap on the center tab to enter camera mode. From here you will be brought to a creative space to make a video. You can choose between making it 15seconds or 60seconds long. On the right side of the screen there is 6 icons. Flip: This flips the camera direction Speed: Allows you to record your video in slow motion or sped up Beauty: This enables a filter that hides blemishes and smooths out your skin Filters: This lets you change the color filter of the camera. Timer: Set a timer if you want to film something hands-free. Flash: This turns the flash on and off.
