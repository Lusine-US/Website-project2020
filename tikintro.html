Introduction

History of TikTok

Tik tok is one of the most popular applications used currently Tik tok was created by ByteDance and was founded in 2012. Formally know as Musical.ly The App was created by Zhang Yiming.

Rebranded

In November 2017 the App Musical.ly was updated the name was changed to Tik Tok and there was a new layout and algorithm that let users discover new videos based on their likings. The main difference between the two apps is the algorithm. Tik toks new algorithm makes binge watching very easy. It is the modern day equivalent to the app Vine.

Tik-Tok is a social media platform for creating, sharing and discovering short music videos.The app musical.ly was used by young people as an outlet to express themselves through dancing, singing and comedy. The TikTok app allows users to create 15 second videos, soundtracks by music clips. They can also connect clips together to create videos up to 60 seconds long. Tik-Tok is available in over 150 countries it has over 1 billion users, and has been downloaded over 123 million times in the United States alone.

Tik Tok Statistic

Tik Tok is the fifth most used app in 2019. 1.5 billion downloads on the App Store and Google Play in 2019. India 466.8,China 173.2,USA 123.8 downloads. Users on Tik Tok spend an average of 52 minutes per day. Tik Tok is available in 155 countries.
