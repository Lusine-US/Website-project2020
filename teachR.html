Teaching and Learning with Reddit

Although Reddit may not be the go-to for Learning and Teaching in a classroom bases, it could work. Social Media now a days is a major part in our society. It connects on another to discuss share and collaborate our ideas and criticisms. The way it could be used for Teaching and Learning in a classroom environment or even to expand your general knowledge on a subject is growing each year, such as it can make it easier to share class materials, can influence discussion on certain topics, and make working together easier than it ever has been before.

The first way it could be used is when a teacher creates a reddit page for his/her students, to inform them with various assignments that may be given in that class and the same with homework. This works because the students can comment on any quires about the work that has been given and the teacher can give feedback.

The second way it could be used is for discussions on various topics that might have been discussed in class, during that teaching. Students can communicate with one another in the comments section expressing their opinions on whatever topic.

The third way it could be used for learning purposes is, people can post about various events that may be occurring globally, for instance the corona virus. It has been trending worldwide and people are uncertain about how they should react to it.
