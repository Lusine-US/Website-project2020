Cautions and Warnings!

With any Social Media application there are multiple safety aspects to it to protect people online from bullying or harassment and racism online. Of course, social media apps don’t allow you to be a troll online and there are multiple ways in which they can be stopped. Some challenges people might face is the fact that reddit can be quite brutal with its trolls because of the amount of freedom they have, and it is an application to let people have their own opinions.

Although there is a block and report button there this isn’t stopping people from making new accounts and trolling people over and over. Reddit does have an age restriction of 13 years, so that means anyone under that age cannot sign up to Reddit

But when you sign up to reddit it doesn’t ask you for your date of birth or anything, so this means anyone is free to sign up to reddit and do what they please, this leading to multiple accounts just there for trolling.

As soon as someone posts a query or literally anything on reddit there could be many people that disagree with it and will throw abuse towards that person, and the worst-case scenario is that the person gets their account removed from reddit. For privacy and safety, you can change a number of different things in this tab for instance. You can set what you want to see on your reddit search bar etc. This making it better for the user to see what he/she wants to see.
